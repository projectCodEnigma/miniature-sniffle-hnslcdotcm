What to Expect: Our Onboarding Process

A clear, step-by-step process to get your Google Ads campaigns up and running efficiently.

1

Discovery Call

We'll discuss your business goals, current marketing performance, target audience, and budget. This helps us understand your needs and determine if we're a good fit to work together.

2

Account Access & Initial Audit

You'll grant access to your Google Ads account and relevant platforms. We'll conduct a comprehensive audit of your existing campaigns, identifying opportunities, underperforming areas, and potential quick wins.

3

Audit Report & Roadmap

You'll receive a comprehensive audit report with our findings, recommendations, and a clear roadmap outlining the recommended approach, key focus areas, and expected improvements. This gives you a complete picture of what we'll work on together.

4

Payment & Service Agreement

Payment is required before we proceed with detailed setup and strategy work. You'll sign a service agreement with me and my company (ZELDIGITAL® Marketing Services OPC) as your service provider. This formalizes our working relationship and ensures we can allocate the necessary time and resources to your account.

5

Complete Setup: Tracking, Integrations & Optimization

We'll implement proper tracking, conversion tracking, and all necessary integrations. This includes optimizing your account structure, setting up conversion goals, configuring Google Analytics, and ensuring all tracking pixels and integrations are properly implemented.

6

Detailed Strategy & Campaign Launch Plan

We'll finalize the detailed strategy document outlining campaign structure, targeting approach, budget allocation, and expected timelines. We'll review this together and make any adjustments before launch.

7

Campaign Launch & Initial Optimization

We'll build and launch your campaigns according to the approved strategy. During the first few weeks, we'll closely monitor performance and make rapid optimizations to improve results.

8

Ongoing Management & Reporting

You'll receive weekly updates and monthly comprehensive reports showing performance metrics, ROI analysis, and strategic recommendations. We'll continuously optimize campaigns to maximize your results. Ongoing service fees are billed monthly to maintain continuous campaign management.

Important Disclaimer: Past performance is not a guarantee of future results. Google Ads campaign performance can vary significantly based on numerous factors including but not limited to: market competition, geographic location, advertising cost competition, seasonal trends, industry changes, Google algorithm updates, quality score fluctuations, competitor actions, economic conditions, and changes in consumer behavior. While we use proven strategies and best practices to optimize your campaigns, actual results may differ from past performance metrics shown. We commit to providing professional management and optimization services, but cannot guarantee specific ROI, conversion rates, or cost-per-acquisition outcomes.
