Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Things people ask before we start working together.

What ad spend level makes sense for working together?

The audit works for any spend level $3K+/mo. The retainer is most impactful for accounts spending $10K–$500K/mo where the cost of bad decisions is real.

Do you work with agencies or only direct clients?

Both. I offer white-label fulfillment for agencies that need senior-level Google Ads execution without hiring full-time. Fully confidential.

Can you manage Facebook or LinkedIn Ads too?

No. I specialize in Google Ads ecosystem only — Search, PMax, Shopping, YouTube, Demand Gen. Deep beats wide in paid acquisition.

What if my tracking is completely broken?

That's actually a common starting point. The Tracking Build package is designed exactly for that — we establish clean measurement before optimizing spend.

Do you require a long-term contract?

Retainer engagements have a 3-month minimum. After that, month-to-month. Projects and audits are one-time with no ongoing commitment.

How do you report results?

Weekly async reports via a shared Looker Studio dashboard, plus monthly video walkthroughs. No vanity metrics — just the numbers that drive decisions.

What's your timezone and availability?

Cebu City (PHT, UTC+8). I work with clients across APAC, Australia, UK, and the US. Async-first with scheduled calls in your timezone.

Can I start with the audit and move to a retainer?

Yes — and that's the recommended path. The audit gives us both a clear picture of the account before committing to an ongoing engagement.
