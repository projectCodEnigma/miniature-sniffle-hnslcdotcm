Common questions.

What's included in the initial audit?
Full account teardown: campaign structure analysis, conversion tracking validation (GA4, GTM, server-side setup), Performance Max asset review, search term waste audit, bidding strategy assessment, and a prioritized fix roadmap with 30 days of hands-on execution to implement the highest-impact changes.
How do you calculate the 15% management fee?
It's 15% of total monthly ad spend across all managed platforms (Google Ads, Microsoft Ads, etc.), with a floor of $4,500/month. So if you're spending $30K/month, the fee is $4,500. If you're spending $50K/month, it's $7,500. The retainer scales with spend because the optimization workload scales with budget.
What's the difference between Growth and Fractional Director?
Growth tier is pure execution — I own your Google Ads account and optimize it continuously. Fractional Director is strategic leadership: multi-channel planning, team integration, vendor oversight, custom reporting infrastructure, and quarterly business planning. It's for companies that need acquisition infrastructure ownership at the executive level.
Do you work with agencies or only direct clients?
I work with both. For agencies, I typically come in as a white-label specialist or fractional Google Ads director to handle technical execution, tracking fixes, or Performance Max buildouts. For direct clients, I operate as your dedicated acquisition system owner.
What industries do you not work with?
I don't work with: crypto, gambling, adult content, MLM/network marketing, or political campaigns. My expertise is in eCommerce, healthcare/medspa, B2B SaaS, and high-intent lead generation for service businesses.
How quickly can we start?
After the fit call and agreement, onboarding takes 3–5 business days (account access, tracking audit, initial setup). Week 1 is diagnostic and planning. Active optimization starts Week 2. For audits, you'll have the full report and execution plan within 10 business days.
What if I'm already working with an agency?
Common scenario. I can either: (1) take over completely if you're looking to transition, (2) work alongside your agency as a specialist for tracking/Performance Max/technical optimization, or (3) conduct an independent audit to give you clarity on what's actually happening in your account. The fit call will determine the right structure.
