Common questions

Why is there a $5,000/month minimum ad spend?

Below $5K, the data volume is too thin to optimize meaningfully. This threshold ensures there's enough signal to make decisions that actually compound over time — and that the engagement is worthwhile for both sides.

Do you track hours?

No. The fee is outcome-based, not hour-based. What matters is campaign performance, not how much time was logged in a tracker.

How fast is onboarding?

Most accounts are live within 7 days of the fit call. Conversion tracking, campaign structure, and reporting setup happen in the first week.

Who do you work with?

U.S.-based ecommerce brands, DTC operators, and B2B lead generation businesses. eCommerce, direct-to-consumer, and service businesses with proven product-market fit.

Is there a long-term contract?

Engagements are structured for 90-day minimum cycles — enough runway to build systems that produce results. After that, month-to-month.
