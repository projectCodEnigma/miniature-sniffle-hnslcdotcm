Common questions

Things people ask
before buying.

Do I need to be spending a certain amount for this to be worth it?
The minimum where this makes financial sense is around $2,000/month in ad spend. Below that, the audit fee is a disproportionately large percentage of your budget. Above $2K, a single fix — a wasted keyword category, a broken conversion action, a PMax campaign eating your brand budget — typically recovers the $397 within the first week.
What access do you need to my account?
Read-only access to your Google Ads account and your GA4 property. You add me as a Standard User (read-only) in Google Ads and as a Viewer in GA4. I never need your login credentials, and you can remove access immediately after delivery.
What if I'm currently working with an agency or freelancer?
This audit is actually most useful in that situation. It gives you an independent operator's read on what's actually happening in your account — without your current provider knowing. Many clients use it to decide whether to stay, renegotiate, or switch. I'll tell you honestly if the current setup is solid.
Is the $397 applied toward ongoing management?
Yes. If we work together after the audit, the $397 is credited in full against the first month of management. It's not a discount — it's credited as a deposit. The audit is a legitimate standalone product, not a bait-and-switch for upselling.
What if I disagree with the findings?
Then we talk about it. I'm not delivering a one-size-fits-all template — I'm giving you my operator read based on what I actually see in your account. If you have context I don't, the follow-up call is the right place to work through it. I'd rather you push back and we get to the truth than have you nod along with something that doesn't fit your business.
How is this different from a free audit other agencies offer?
Free audits are sales documents. They're designed to make your account look broken so you hire the agency. This audit is a paid deliverable — my incentive is to give you accurate findings, not to manufacture urgency. The Loom walkthrough and written action plan are usable whether you hire me or not.
