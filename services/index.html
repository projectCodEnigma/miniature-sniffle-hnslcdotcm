Common Questions

FAQs

Do you replace our agency or work alongside them?

+

Either. I can lead, collaborate, or transition — whatever gets you to ROI fastest. Many clients use me to audit and direct an existing agency while building toward full ownership.

How fast can we see results?

+

Depends on signal quality and spend, but the first 2–4 weeks typically unlock wins from structure, tracking, and creative fixes. Compounding gains build from there.

Do you handle creative?

+

Yes — ad copy and creative concepts are included. For heavier production, I'll direct your team or bring in trusted partners. Creative is always built to serve conversion, not aesthetics.

Can you build landing pages?

+

Yes — conversion-focused pages and iterative testing come with management engagements or as a standalone project scoped to your goals.

What about compliance and data privacy?

+

Event design respects consent requirements and I'll align with your legal/IT where needed. Consent-mode v2 and cookieless-ready setups are standard practice.
