Operating Principles

How I Operate

This page explains how I think, how I work, and who I am not a fit for. If this resonates, we’ll build systems that compound together.

My Role

I operate paid acquisition systems for founders scaling eCommerce, healthcare/medspa, B2B SaaS, and high-intent lead generation businesses. Not as a vendor. Not as a campaign manager. I take ownership of the full infrastructure — so you don’t have to wrestle with broken tracking, confusing Performance Max campaigns, or unreliable data signals.

What I Take Ownership Of

  • • Full acquisition infrastructure: campaign architecture, Performance Max setup & auditing, and channel-level signals
  • • Bulletproof conversion tracking and measurement (server-side tagging, enhanced conversions, offline import, GA4 accuracy)
  • • First-party data strategy as your new competitive moat (Customer Match, consent-based audiences, CLV/profit signals)
  • • Budget discipline, downside protection, and clear scaling decisions
  • • Systems that deliver predictable ROAS even as Google’s AI handles execution
  • • Vertical-specific frameworks for eCommerce, healthcare compliance, B2B pipeline attribution, and high-CPC verticals

What I Do Not Do

  • • I do not push buttons in automated campaigns without fixing the underlying structure and data foundation
  • • I do not run Performance Max “set and forget” campaigns that leak budget through redundancy or weak signals
  • • I do not chase vanity metrics or deliver pretty dashboards instead of real business outcomes
  • • I do not attend weekly status calls for reassurance — results are reported clearly and early
  • • I do not work with teams that avoid hard conversations about broken tracking or poor data quality

How Decisions Are Made

Every decision starts with clean data. I prioritize fixing tracking and first-party signals first, then architect Performance Max campaigns with focused asset groups and strong audience signals. Downside protection comes before upside expansion. If something is broken — whether it’s PMax redundancy, incomplete offline conversions, or weak consent modeling — it is addressed directly and early.

Reporting for Owners

You receive clear, founder-focused reporting: actual spend, tracked revenue/pipeline, true ROAS/CPA, data quality health, and trajectory. No inflated dashboards. You see exactly where budget is flowing and whether the systems (tracking + PMax + first-party data) are compounding.

Who This Is a Fit For

  • • Founders and owners who want predictable, high-ROAS acquisition systems — not constant campaign tweaks
  • • Businesses spending $10K+/month where clean tracking and first-party data directly impact cash flow and scaling
  • • Teams in eCommerce, healthcare/medspa, B2B SaaS, legal, finance, or home services that value technical depth and vertical expertise
  • • Operators who understand that systems (not heroics) create sustainable growth

Who This Is Not a Fit For

  • • Marketing teams looking only for tactical execution or low-level campaign management
  • • Businesses unwilling to invest in proper tracking infrastructure and first-party data
  • • Teams that need constant hand-holding or prefer hype over honest performance reviews
  • • Anyone optimizing for optics or short-term vanity metrics instead of long-term compounding systems
