How I Work

I don't just manage ads. I build paid acquisition infrastructure that gets smarter over time.

01

Fix the foundation first

Before touching campaigns: audit your conversion tracking, implement server-side tagging if needed, fix GA4/GTM setup, establish proper attribution models. Most agencies skip this because it's hard. I start here because campaigns built on bad data never scale.

02

Architect campaigns with AI bidding in mind

Performance Max, Demand Gen, and automated bidding aren't threats — they're leverage when structured correctly. I build account architecture with proper segmentation, feed the algorithm quality signals, and use first-party audiences to guide machine learning toward profitable outcomes.

03

Build first-party data moats

Customer Match isn't a feature — it's a competitive advantage. I help you activate CRM data, build high-value audience segments, implement enhanced conversions, and create remarketing systems that turn your customer list into an acquisition engine.

04

Operate like you're on the cap table

I don't optimize for vanity metrics. Every dollar needs to defend its ROI. I focus on contribution margin, customer LTV, payback period, and incrementality — the metrics that actually matter to your business, not just your ad account.
