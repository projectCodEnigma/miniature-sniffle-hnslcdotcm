Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a tracking implementation typically take? +

For a basic GA4 + GTM setup: 3–4 weeks. Complex implementations with server-side tracking and CRM integrations: 6–8 weeks. Each project is custom scoped during discovery.

Do you manage the actual Google Ads campaigns, or just tracking? +

Both. Tracking is the foundation, but I also manage campaign optimization, bidding, and scaling based on clean conversion data. The two work together to maximize ROI.

What if we're currently using [Agency/Tool]? +

No problem. I can work alongside your current setup or take over completely. We'll audit what's working and what isn't, then move forward from there.

Is this for SaaS, e-commerce, B2B, or all industries? +

All, with special expertise in SaaS, e-commerce, B2B/manufacturing, local services, and lead gen. Complex funnel businesses see the most value from proper tracking.

What if we don't have a CRM or backend system? +

We can still set up front-end conversion tracking, but the ROI is limited. I'd recommend establishing a basic CRM or data system first—it's fundamental to profitability.
