FAQ

Questions about
Measurement

How long does a full tracking implementation take? +
Typical implementations take 4–6 weeks from audit to full deployment. This includes GA4 setup, server-side GTM configuration, conversion tagging, offline data integration, and comprehensive testing. The timeline depends on your system complexity and internal resource availability.
What happens if I already have GA4 set up? +
I'll audit your existing setup, identify misconfigurations, and either optimize or rebuild it. Most GA4 implementations I audit have 30–50% event tracking issues. We fix those, validate the data, and integrate with your acquisition systems.
Can you integrate tracking with our existing CRM? +
Yes. Whether it's Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, or a custom system, I can set up bidirectional sync so Google Ads sees your actual conversions (with margin data if available) and you see performance metrics in your CRM.
What's the difference between server-side and client-side tracking? +
Client-side tracking (pixels) is blocked by ad blockers and user privacy settings, losing 20–40% of conversion data. Server-side tracking is more reliable because it doesn't rely on browser pixels — data flows directly from your server to Google's servers, bypassing privacy restrictions.
Do you manage campaigns after implementation? +
I can if you want. My main focus is building clean measurement infrastructure. But once tracking is solid, I can optimize your campaigns using the accurate data — restructuring for profitability, adjusting bids, refining audiences.
