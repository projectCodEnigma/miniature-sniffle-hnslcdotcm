QUICK ANSWERS.

The most common questions before booking a discovery call.

Is PaidPod a managed service?+
Advisory, not management. Signal and Advisor tiers provide strategy and direction — your team executes. Operator tier includes hands-on campaign builds and restructures.
Can agencies white-label PaidPod?+
Yes — Advisor and Operator tiers include fully unbranded deliverables. No PaidPod or KaizenStrategy branding on client-facing documents. You stay the expert to your clients.
What's the minimum ad spend?+
No hard minimum, but you'll extract the most value at $3,000+/month in Google Ads spend. If you're below that, we'll tell you honestly on the discovery call.
Is there a long-term contract?+
No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel, pause, or upgrade with 14 days notice. No lock-in, no penalties.
Why is it called PaidPod?+
Pod = a dedicated unit with a specific function. Paid = paid media. PaidPod is the paid search unit you plug into your business or agency — focused, contained, effective.
