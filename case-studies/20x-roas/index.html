Background & Objectives

A U.S.-based online security‑equipment retailer needed to scale revenue efficiently and stabilize results across seasonal swings.

Goals:

  • Maximize ROAS
  • Increase conversion revenue
  • Stabilize performance through seasonality

The Challenge

Understanding the starting point and obstacles

Client Profile:

  • Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) brand in the lifestyle/wellness space
  • 3+ years in business with established product line
  • Previous ROAS averaging 2.5x (below industry standard)
  • Monthly ad spend: $12,000-$15,000

Key Problems:

  • • Broad, unfocused targeting
  • • Poorly optimized landing pages
  • • Limited conversion tracking
  • • Inefficient budget allocation
  • • No systematic testing process

The Strategy

How we achieved exceptional results

Phase 1: Comprehensive Audit & Strategy (Week 1-2)

  • • Deep-dive analysis of existing campaigns and performance data
  • • Competitor research and market opportunity identification
  • • Customer journey mapping and conversion path optimization
  • • Technical audit of tracking and measurement setup
Key Finding: 47% of ad spend was going to irrelevant traffic with poor conversion potential

Phase 2: Precision Targeting Implementation (Week 3-4)

  • • Implemented exact and phrase match keywords with tight negative lists
  • • Created high-intent audience segments based on behavior data
  • • Set up Performance Max campaigns with detailed asset groups
  • • Optimized bidding strategies for maximum efficiency
Immediate Impact: CTR improved by 73% and CPC decreased by 35% within 2 weeks

Phase 3: Conversion Rate Optimization (Week 5-8)

  • • Redesigned landing pages with conversion-focused layouts
  • • A/B tested headlines, CTAs, and social proof elements
  • • Implemented advanced conversion tracking and attribution
  • • Optimized checkout flow to reduce cart abandonment
Conversion Boost: Landing page conversion rate increased from 1.8% to 4.2%

Phase 4: Scaling & Optimization (Week 9-12)

  • • Gradually increased budgets on highest-performing campaigns
  • • Launched additional ad variations and expanded winning audiences
  • • Implemented automated bidding with custom conversion goals
  • • Weekly performance reviews and strategic adjustments
Final Result: Achieved peak 20x ROAS while maintaining consistent daily performance
