This client came in with a broken acquisition setup — a 0.17× ROAS that meant they were spending $6 to make back $1. The problem wasn't the product; it was the campaign architecture and signal quality feeding Google's AI.

The fix was a full Performance Max rebuild integrated with YouTube video assets — using product-specific video creatives to train Google's AI on who the real buyers were. Video engagement data from YouTube became the warm audience signal that Shopping and Search campaigns couldn't generate alone.

Combined with clean server-side conversion tracking and Customer Match uploads, the system compounded month over month — hitting a 21.55× peak ROAS during the optimization window.

Why YouTube + PMax

01 /
Video as Audience Signal
YouTube view data tells Google's AI who engaged with your product before they searched. This pre-intent signal is more valuable than keyword targeting alone — it gets Shopping and Search in front of warm buyers.
02 /
Clean Conversion Foundation
Server-side tracking + Enhanced Conversions means Google sees every real purchase — not just browser-tracked events that iOS and ad blockers miss. Accurate signals = accurate bidding = better ROAS.
03 /
PMax Architecture Built Right
Asset groups segmented by product margin, audience signals layered by intent stage, listing groups structured so Google's AI spends where it should. Not the default setup — the engineered one.
04 /
Compounding Signal Loop
YouTube views → Shopping clicks → purchases → Customer Match uploads → better bidding → more reach → more views. Each cycle improves targeting. That's how you go from 0.17× to 21.55× over 12 months.
