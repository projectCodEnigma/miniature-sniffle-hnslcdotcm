Questions

Frequently asked

Full FAQ page →
Do you only work with Philippines-based businesses? +
No — the majority of clients are in the US, Australia, and the UK. Being Philippines-based is a rate advantage for them, and a timezone that still allows for decent overlap, especially with AU/APAC clients.
What's the minimum ad spend you work with? +
$5,000/month USD minimum. Below that, it's difficult to gather enough data to optimize meaningfully — especially with Performance Max campaigns. For smaller budgets, a one-time audit engagement might be a better fit.
How is this different from a Google Ads agency? +
At most agencies, your account is managed by a junior account manager following a process. With Hans, you deal directly with the strategist doing the work — no middlemen, no handoffs. The expertise is consistent because it's always the same person.
Do you require long-term contracts? +
A 90-day minimum is standard — Google Ads needs at least 60–90 days of clean data before you can draw meaningful conclusions and optimize bidding strategies. After that, engagements roll month-to-month with 30 days' notice.
Is server-side tracking setup included? +
Yes — for Growth and Scale tiers, server-side GTM and enhanced conversions are included in onboarding. For Starter, basic GA4 and conversion tracking is set up. sGTM can be added as an add-on for $400 one-time.
What platforms do you work with? +
Primarily Google Ads (Search, Shopping, Performance Max, Display, YouTube). Also experienced with Microsoft/Bing Ads as a secondary channel. Social ad management is not offered — keeping it focused means higher quality on Google.
