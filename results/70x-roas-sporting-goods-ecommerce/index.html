Case Study

70x ROAS for a Sporting Goods Ecommerce Retailer

Google Ads demand capture rebuilt for seasonal scaling across Australia & New Zealand (90 days).

Google Ads · Australia & New Zealand · 90 Days (Sep–Nov 2025)

Peak Performance
70x ROAS

With sustained 60x+ ROAS windows during peak periods.

Timeframe
~90 days

Sep–Nov 2025 (seasonal ramp → peak).

Channel
Google Ads

Search + Performance Max, operated as one system.

1. The situation

  • Fragmented Google Ads campaign structure
  • Weak geographic targeting
  • Limited keyword governance
  • Waste from broad search traffic
  • No buyer avatar alignment
  • Irregular optimisation cadence

2. What changed (operator actions)

  • Rebuilt Search and Performance Max architecture
  • Search term mining and negative keyword system
  • Geo targeting focused on high intent AU East Coast
  • Audience signals aligned to buyer profiles
  • Campaign structure built for BFCM scaling
  • Daily monitoring and optimisation

3. Results

  • Up to 70x peak ROAS
  • Sustained 60x+ ROAS windows during peak periods
  • Major reduction in wasted spend
  • Higher volume of high intent traffic
  • Stable scaling during Black Friday / Cyber Monday

4. Business impact (in plain language)

The account moved from fragmented campaign management to a structured paid acquisition system. The new architecture improved efficiency, stabilized ROAS, and created a foundation for scaling across Australia and New Zealand.
